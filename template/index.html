About Us

Hire Your Geek is an online platform that connects businesses and individuals with skilled freelance software developers from around the world. The platform allows clients to post project requirements, and then receive bids from qualified programmers who are interested in the project. Clients can then choose the freelancer they want to work with, based on factors such as their experience, ratings, and proposed cost. Hire Your Geek provides a range of tools to help clients and freelancers collaborate effectively, including messaging systems, project management tools, and secure payment systems.
